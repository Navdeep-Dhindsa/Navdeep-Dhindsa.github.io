About me

I am a postdoctoral researcher at IMSc Chennai working broadly on Lattice QCD with Dr. M Padmanath. I did my PhD at IISER Mohali under the supervision of Dr. Anosh Joseph. The main theme of my PhD was about the non-perturbative studies of supersymmetric theories using the lattice framework. The calculations were performed for various dimensional theories using Monte Carlo with the help of computations in the MPI framework. My PhD thesis will be available on arXiv in the coming month. Apart from the lattice investigations, I am also interested in other non-perturbative methods to explore strongly interacting field theories. Recently, I have started working on numerical bootstrap to study various Matrix Models. I am also interested in exploring various other advanced computer algorithms.
